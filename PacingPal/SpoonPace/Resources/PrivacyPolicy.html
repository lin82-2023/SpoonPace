Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

SpoonPace collects health and activity data that you voluntarily input into the app, including:

  • Energy usage entries (activities and their energy costs)
  • Symptom logs and severity ratings
  • Daily energy budget settings
  • Health data imported from HealthKit (if you enable it)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Displaying your energy tracking history
  • Generating AI-powered insights (if you have an active subscription)

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data and we do not store your data on any external servers.

4. AI Processing

If you use AI features, aggregated anonymized data is processed by your chosen AI provider (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, DeepSeek, etc.). Only summary statistics are sent for analysis - we never send your personal identifiable information.

5. Your Rights

You can export or delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@spoonpace.app
