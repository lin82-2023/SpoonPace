Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

PacingPal collects health and activity data that you voluntarily input into the app, including:

  • Energy usage entries (activities and their energy costs)
  • Symptom logs and severity ratings
  • Daily energy budget settings

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Displaying your energy tracking history
  • Generating AI-powered insights (if you have an active subscription)
  • Syncing across your devices via iCloud

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your data.

4. AI Processing

If you use AI features, your data is processed by Claude AI (Anthropic). Only anonymized summaries are sent for analysis.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@pacingpal.app
