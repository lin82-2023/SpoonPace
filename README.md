# EnergyBuddy / 量记

> EnergyBuddy - Energy management app for Long COVID and ME/CFS patients.
> 量记 - 为长新冠/慢性疲劳综合症患者设计的能量管理和症状追踪应用。

## 🎯 项目目标

Build a **modern, AI-powered energy pacing app** that solves a real, unmet need for millions of patients.

打造一个**现代化的、AI 赋能的能量 pacing 应用**，解决数百万患者真实未满足的需求。

## 💡 核心问题

Existing apps on the App Store are either:
- Outdated and unmaintained
- Generic health trackers that don't understand pacing
- No AI analytics or insights

App Store 上现有应用要么：
- 过时且没人维护
- 是不理解 pacing 的通用健康追踪器
- 没有 AI 分析和洞察

## ✨ 核心功能

- ✅ **Energy Bucket (Spoon Theory)** - Daily energy budget tracking
- ✅ **Symptom Tracking** - 0-10 severity logging
- ✅ **Calendar History** - Browse historical data by date
- ✅ **AI Natural Language Input** - Type naturally, AI structures your data
- ✅ **AI Weekly Summary** - AI analyzes your energy patterns weekly
- ✅ **HealthKit Integration** - Import steps, active energy, sleep data
- ✅ **Privacy First** - All personal data stored locally on device

## 🎨 命名

**English:** EnergyBuddy
- Easy to remember, clearly communicates what it does
- "Energy" is the core concept we manage
- "Buddy" = friendly helper that's always with you

**中文：量记**
- 记录能量，简洁好记
- 符合产品调性

Alternative names: EnergyPulse, PaceGuard, SpoonPlan / 能量脉搏, 守步, 勺记

## 🌎 Target Markets

1. **Primary**: North America & Europe - large patient base
2. **Secondary**: Hong Kong & Chinese-speaking Southeast Asia
