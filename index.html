EnergyBuddy is an energy management app for Long COVID and ME/CFS patients. Track your daily energy budget with the spoon theory, get AI-powered insights, and understand your body's patterns.

量记（EnergyBuddy）是为长新冠和慢性疲劳综合症（ME/CFS）患者设计的能量管理应用。 使用勺子理论追踪每日能量预算，获得 AI 驱动的洞察，了解你身体的模式。

Core Features 核心功能

🪣

Energy Bucket 能量桶

Visualize your daily energy budget based on the spoon theory. Track what activities consume your energy in real-time.

基于勺子理论可视化你的每日能量预算。实时追踪哪些活动消耗了你的能量。

🤖

AI-Powered Insights AI 驱动洞察

Get weekly AI analysis of your energy patterns. Discover what activities trigger your symptoms.

每周获得 AI 对你能量模式的分析。发现哪些活动触发了你的症状。

💬

Natural Language Input 自然语言输入

Just type what you did in plain language. AI automatically extracts activities and energy cost.

只需用自然语言输入你做了什么。AI 自动提取活动和能量消耗。

📅

Calendar History 日历历史

Browse your past entries by date. Look back and understand how your energy changes over time.

按日期浏览历史记录。回顾并了解你的能量如何随时间变化。

❤️

Symptom Tracking 症状追踪

Track fatigue, pain, sleep quality and other symptoms on a 0-10 scale. Correlate with energy usage.

用 0-10 分追踪疲劳、疼痛、睡眠质量等症状。与能量消耗进行关联分析。

🔒

Privacy First 隐私优先

All your personal health data stays on your device. Only AI analysis is sent securely to the cloud.

所有你的个人健康数据都保存在你的设备上。只有 AI 分析会加密发送到云端。
