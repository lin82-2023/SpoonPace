## App Description (English)

# EnergyBuddy - Energy Management for Long COVID & ME/CFS

EnergyBuddy helps you manage your energy levels using the proven "spoon theory" energy bucket model, designed specifically for people living with Long COVID, ME/CFS, fibromyalgia, and other chronic illnesses that cause fatigue.

### Why EnergyBuddy?

Living with a chronic illness means carefully managing your limited energy every day. EnergyBuddy helps you:

• Track your daily energy expenditure with a simple tap
• Visualize your remaining energy bucket at a glance
• Log symptoms and their severity over time
• Get personalized AI-powered weekly insights
• Sync with Apple Health for automatic step count, activity, and sleep data
• Plan your activities to avoid post-exertional malaise
• All data stays on your device - private by design

### Features:

✓ **Spoon Theory Energy Bucket** - Visual representation of your daily energy budget
✓ **Quick Entry** - Add activities and symptoms in seconds with preset energy costs
✓ **AI Natural Language Input** - Simply describe what you did in plain text, AI extracts activities and energy cost automatically
✓ **Weekly AI Insights** - Get personalized observations and gentle suggestions based on your patterns
✓ **Apple Health Sync** - Automatically import steps, active energy, sleep duration, and resting heart rate
✓ **Calendar History** - View your historical data and spot patterns over time
✓ **Privacy First** - All your health data stays on your device, never uploaded to third-party servers without your explicit consent
✓ **Calming Design** - Soft colors and clean interface designed to reduce eye strain and cognitive load

EnergyBuddy isn't about pushing you to do more - it's about helping you understand your limits and make informed decisions to stay well. Knowledge is power, and by understanding your energy patterns, you can better manage your condition and improve your quality of life.
